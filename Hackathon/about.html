Virginia COVID-19 Data Visualization

About

This web page is made to show people the effects that COVID-19 has had on the population of the state of Virginia. The main topics that are focused on are the effects covid has had on race/ethnicity and colleges/universities. The website also focuses on vaccination status and vaccine doses administered. The mission of this website is to provide an easy way for users to visualize and understand COVID-19 data.

Note: All data is from June 15, 2021 to April 8, 2022, except for data on vaccine doses administered, which is from May 1, 2021 to April 8, 2022.
